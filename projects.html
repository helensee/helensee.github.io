FoodSNAP

Goal: Provide an application that allows food related groups, supermarkets or restaurants, to make event times for people that are part of SNAP. The application would also allow Non-profit organization volunteers to help during the distribution of food during the event.

How it's made: The key pieces that builds this application is MySQL, Amazon Web Services, Node.js, Express.js, and Bootstrap

My build: I focused on back-end since my main skill set is databases and manipulating the data. I designed the application and decided on the main functionalities.
